About Hyde Park

Historic Hyde Park is one of Boise's most vibrant districts, located along North 13th Street. It blends historic charm with modern shops, restaurants, and community life.

History & Heritage

Dating back to the early 1900s, Hyde Park features landmarks like the Waymire Building and Odd Fellows Building. It developed as part of Boise’s early suburban expansion and reflects the streetcar era.

Things to Do

  • Explore local shops and cafes
  • Visit restaurants and bakeries
  • Access Boise Foothills trails
  • Enjoy a walk through historic streets

Hyde Park Street Fair

The Hyde Park Street Fair is a major annual event featuring music, art, food vendors, and community activities that attract thousands of visitors each year.
