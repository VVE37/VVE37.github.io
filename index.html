Historic Hyde Park

The Heart of Boise's North End

About Hyde Park

Historic Hyde Park blends historic charm with modern shops, restaurants, and community life. Located along North 13th Street, it remains one of Boise's most vibrant districts.

History & Heritage

Early 1900s

Hyde Park developed during Boise’s early expansion and reflects historic architecture.

Streetcar Era

Electric streetcars helped shape Hyde Park into a connected suburban hub.

Preservation

Buildings like the Waymire and Odd Fellows were restored to keep history alive.

Things to Do

Dining

Enjoy local cafes, bakeries, and restaurants full of character.

Shopping

Explore unique boutiques and local artisan shops.

Outdoors

Access the Boise Foothills trails and enjoy scenic walks.
